## Data derived from Aesthetics Wiki / Fandom

Aesthetics-KG is derived in part from pages of the Aesthetics Wiki on Fandom:
https://aesthetics.fandom.com/

Unless otherwise stated, text on Fandom communities is licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported License.

Accordingly, data fields derived from Aesthetics Wiki content are distributed under CC BY-SA-compatible terms.

For each extracted aesthetic page, we provide provenance metadata including:

- source page title;
- source page URL;
- extraction date;
- source wiki;
- applicable source license;
- local entity URI;
- extracted fields used in RDF generation.

Reusers are responsible for complying with the applicable source licenses, including attribution and ShareAlike requirements.

The released KG does not redistribute Fandom media files. No assumption is made that images or other non-text media from Fandom are covered by the same license as wiki text.
